Android Testing Instructions

Mobile Camera Scanner and PDF Download Functionality
GreenPay Papua New Guinea - Buy Online Feature

Prerequisites

Before beginning the test, please ensure you have the following:

  • An Android smartphone with a working camera
  • A physical passport containing an MRZ (Machine Readable Zone - the two printed lines at the bottom of the passport)
  • A well-lit environment with minimal shadows
  • Internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)

Test Procedure

Step 1: Access the Application

  1. Open your preferred web browser on your Android phone (Chrome, Firefox, or Samsung Internet)
  2. Navigate to the following URL: https://greenpay.eywademo.cloud/buy-online
  3. Wait for the page to load completely

Step 2: Initiate Camera Scanner

  1. Locate and tap the button labeled "Scan Passport with Camera"
  2. When prompted, grant camera permission to your browser
  3. The camera interface will open, displaying a live view from your phone's camera

Step 3: Capture Passport Image

  1. Position the passport in a well-lit area, avoiding direct sunlight that may cause glare
  2. Ensure the entire passport is visible within the camera frame
  3. Hold the device steady and tap the "Capture Image" button
  4. The system will process the image for approximately 5-10 seconds
Note: The OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology reads the MRZ lines at the bottom of the passport. These two lines contain encoded information about the passport holder. For best results, ensure these lines are clearly visible and in focus.

Step 4: Verify Extracted Information

After processing, the form should automatically populate with the following information. Please verify each field:

Field What to Check
Passport Number Should match the passport exactly (typically 9 digits)
Surname Should match the family name on the passport
Given Name Should show first and middle names with proper spacing (e.g., "JOHN DAVID" not "JOHNDAVID"). There should be no random letters at the end.
Nationality Should display the full country name (e.g., "Bulgaria", "United States", "Denmark") rather than a three-letter code
Date of Birth Should match the passport in YYYY-MM-DD format
Sex Should display either "Male" or "Female"

Step 5: Complete Payment Process

  1. Review all auto-populated information for accuracy
  2. Tap the "Continue to Payment" button
  3. You will be redirected to the Stripe payment gateway
  4. Enter the following test card details:
    • Card Number: 4242 4242 4242 4242
    • Expiry Date: Any future date (example: 12/34)
    • CVC: Any three digits (example: 123)
    • ZIP Code: Any five digits (example: 12345)
  5. Complete the payment and wait to be redirected to the success page

Step 6: Test PDF Download Functionality

On the payment success page, you will test the voucher PDF download feature:

  1. Locate and tap the "Download PDF" button
  2. Observe the behavior of your device. One of the following should occur:
    • Scenario A: The Android Share Sheet appears, allowing you to choose "Save to Files", "Downloads", or share via other applications
    • Scenario B: The PDF downloads directly to your Downloads folder without prompting
    • Scenario C: Your browser displays a prompt asking "Download voucher-XXXXX.pdf?"
  3. After the download completes, verify the file:
    • Open your Files app or Downloads folder
    • Locate the file named "voucher-XXXXX.pdf" (where XXXXX is the voucher code)
    • Open the PDF file and confirm it contains:
      • A QR code
      • The voucher code
      • Your passport details
      • An expiry date

Step 7: Alternative Email Test (Optional)

If PDF download does not work on your device, test the email delivery option:

  1. Tap the "Email Voucher" button on the success page
  2. Enter a valid email address in the dialog box
  3. Tap "Send" and wait for confirmation
  4. Check your email inbox for the voucher PDF attachment
  5. Verify the PDF opens correctly and contains all required information

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Camera fails to activate

  • Check that camera permission has been granted in your browser settings
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Verify you are accessing the site via HTTPS (secure connection)
  • Try using a different browser

OCR cannot read the passport

  • Improve lighting conditions - avoid shadows and excessive glare
  • Hold the passport flat against a contrasting background
  • Ensure the MRZ lines (bottom two lines) are completely visible and in focus
  • Clean the camera lens if it appears smudged
  • Attempt the capture process again

PDF download does not function

  • Use the "Email Voucher" option as an alternative
  • The PDF will be delivered to your email inbox
  • This method works reliably regardless of browser or device configuration

Extracted data contains errors

  • If given names still contain random letters, please document the exact text displayed
  • Note which fields are incorrect
  • Try scanning the passport again with improved lighting
